Privacy Policy

Last updated: January 24, 2026

1. Introduction

Welcome to Aklaa. We respect your privacy and are committed to protecting your personal data. This privacy policy explains how we collect, use, share and protect your personal information when you use our services.

2. Data Controller

Aklaa
For questions about this privacy policy, you can contact us through the contact details on our website.

3. What data do we collect?

3.1 Data you provide to us

  • Account information: First name, last name, email address and password (stored encrypted)
  • Profile information: Language preference and other optional profile data
  • Usage data: Dishes you add, grocery lists you create, and your shopping cart

3.2 Automatically collected data

  • Technical data: IP address, browser type, device information
  • Usage statistics: How you use our application, which pages you visit
  • Cookies: For authentication and preferences (see Cookie Policy)

4. How do we use your data?

We use your data for the following purposes:

  • Service delivery: To give you access to your account and our features
  • Personalization: To customize your experience to your preferences (e.g. language)
  • Communication: To inform you about important updates or changes
  • Security: To protect your account against unauthorized access
  • Improvement: To analyze and improve our services
  • Legal obligations: To comply with applicable laws and regulations

5. Legal basis for processing

We process your personal data based on:

  • Contract performance: For providing our services
  • Consent: For specific processing for which we ask your consent
  • Legitimate interest: For security and improvement of our services
  • Legal obligation: When required by law

6. Data sharing

We never sell your personal data. We may share your data with:

  • Service providers: Hosting providers, email services (under strict confidentiality)
  • Legal authorities: When legally required or to protect our rights
  • Business transfer: In case of merger, acquisition or sale of business units

7. Data security

We take appropriate technical and organizational measures to protect your data:

  • Encrypted storage of passwords (bcrypt hashing)
  • HTTPS encryption for all communication
  • Regular security updates
  • Access controls and authentication
  • Backups for data recovery

8. Retention period

We do not keep your data longer than necessary:

  • Account data: As long as your account is active
  • Grocery lists: Expired lists are automatically deleted after 1 month
  • Log files: Maximum 90 days
  • After account deletion: Permanently deleted within 30 days

9. Your rights

Under the GDPR you have the following rights:

  • Right of access: You can view and export your data via your account settings
  • Right to rectification: You can modify your data in your profile
  • Right to erasure: You can delete your account and data
  • Right to restriction: You can request restriction of processing
  • Right to data portability: You can export your data
  • Right to object: You can object to certain processing
  • Right to withdraw consent: You can always withdraw consent

To exercise these rights, contact us or use the features in your account.

10. Cookies

We use essential cookies for:

  • Authentication and session management
  • Storing your language preference
  • Security purposes

You can manage cookies through your browser settings, but this may affect functionality.

11. Children

Our services are not directed to persons under 16 years of age. We do not knowingly collect data from children. If you suspect that we have collected data from a child, please contact us.

12. International transfer

Your data may be stored and processed in the European Union. For transfers to countries outside the EU, we ensure appropriate safeguards in accordance with the GDPR.

13. Changes to this policy

We may update this privacy policy from time to time. We will communicate important changes via email. The date at the top of this policy indicates when it was last updated.

14. Contact and complaints

For questions about this privacy policy or to exercise your rights, you can contact us.

You also have the right to file a complaint with the Data Protection Authority if you believe that we are not handling your data correctly.
